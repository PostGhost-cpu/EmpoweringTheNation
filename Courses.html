Courses

At Empowering the Nation, we offer a range of practical, hands-on training courses designed to equip individuals with the skills they need to find work, grow in their careers, or start their own small businesses. Whether you're looking to gain confidence in the kitchen, learn to care for children, sew garments, or maintain a garden — we have a course for you. Explore a range of courses carefully designed to help you gain practical skills, whether through an in-depth 6-month program or a fast-tracked 6-week boost.


Each course is affordable, easy to follow, and taught by experienced trainers who understand real-life challenges. Browse our course options below and take the first step toward building a better future.

Empowering the Nation offers the following six months courses. The following are the detalis of each:



First Aid

Learn how to respond to medical emergencies with confidence. This course covers basic life support, CPR, treating wounds, burns, fractures, and managing emergency scenes—perfect for caregivers and home workers.

Sewing

Master essential sewing techniques from machine setup to garment design. Learn to sew buttons, hems, zips, and perform clothing alterations — great for starting a small tailoring business or enhancing job skills.

Landscaping

Gain practical knowledge to design and care for beautiful gardens. This course covers indigenous and exotic plants, garden layout, built-in features like fountains, and balancing garden aesthetics.

Life Skills

Learn the basics of daily adult life — including opening a bank account, understanding your labour rights, and improving your reading, writing, and number skills for work and independence.

We also offer the following six week short courses. The following are the details of each courses:



Child Minding

Get trained in caring for babies and toddlers, from newborn to age three. Learn about feeding, hygiene, routines, and educational toys — ideal for aspiring nannies or parents.

Cooking

Prepare tasty, nutritious meals on a budget. This course covers balanced diets, meal planning, cooking techniques, and recipes to feed a family healthily every day.

Garden Maintenance

Understand how to care for home gardens with practical skills in watering, pruning, and planting. Learn about plant types, watering rules, and how to grow thriving outdoor spaces.
