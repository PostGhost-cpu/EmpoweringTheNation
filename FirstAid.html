Courses

At Empowering the Nation, we offer a range of practical, hands-on training courses designed to equip individuals with the skills they need to find work, grow in their careers, or start their own small businesses. Whether you're looking to gain confidence in the kitchen, learn to care for children, sew garments, or maintain a garden — we have a course for you.


Empowering the Nation offers the following six months courses. The following are the detalis of each:

  • Ideal for: Learners who want an in-depth, comprehensive understanding of their chosen subject.
  • Structure: Covers all core topics with additional time for practical projects, assessments, and mentorship sessions.

    • Benefits:

  • Gradual learning pace for better retention.
  • More opportunities for hands-on practice.
  • Extended support from instructors.
  • Outcome: A strong, well-rounded skill set and a professional-level portfolio.

    • First Aid

    Course Description:

    This course provides essential life-saving skills that can be used at home or in the workplace. You will learn how to confidently respond to accidents and emergencies, including how to assess an emergency scene, treat burns, fractures, wounds, and bleeding, and perform CPR. You’ll also learn how to assist people experiencing respiratory distress or choking.


    How This Course Helps You:

  • Equips you with skills to react quickly and calmly in emergencies
  • Increases your value as a domestic worker, nanny, or caregiver
  • Builds trust with employers or clients by showing health and safety awareness
  • Boosts your confidence in looking after children, elders, or sick individuals

    • Fees R1500

    Job Opportunities:

  • Domestic worker with safety skills
  • Nanny or childminder
  • Elder caregiver
  • School or daycare assistant
  • Community health volunteer
