Courses

At Empowering the Nation, we offer a range of practical, hands-on training courses designed to equip individuals with the skills they need to find work, grow in their careers, or start their own small businesses. Whether you're looking to gain confidence in the kitchen, learn to care for children, sew garments, or maintain a garden — we have a course for you.


We also offer the following six week short courses. The following are the details of each courses:

  • Ideal for: Learners seeking a fast-track learning experience.
  • Structure: Focused and intensive lessons, aimed at delivering essential knowledge and skills in the shortest possible time.

    • Benefits:

  • Quickly acquire targeted skills for immediate use.
  • Flexible schedule for those balancing other commitments.
  • Perfect for skill upgrades or preparing for a specific project.
  • Outcome: Solid foundational skills and the ability to apply them in real-world scenarios.

    • Child Minding

    Course Description:

    Learn how to safely and lovingly care for babies and toddlers. You’ll study the needs of children from birth to three years old, including feeding, hygiene, sleep schedules, play, and emotional support. You’ll also explore educational toys and basic child development.


    How This Course Helps You:

  • Become a more confident nanny or domestic helper
  • Understand child development and safety
  • Support working parents by providing trustworthy care
  • Build a foundation for childcare-related careers

    • Fees R750

    Job Opportunities:

  • Nanny or au pair
  • Babysitter
  • Crèche assistant
  • Domestic worker with childcare duties
  • Aftercare or school helper
