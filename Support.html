Discount Policy

At Empowering the Nation, we believe in making skills development accessible and affordable. To encourage learners to enroll in multiple courses and grow their skill sets, we offer discounts based on the number of courses selected. Below is our official discount policy:

Number of Courses Enrolled Discount Applied
1 course No discount
2 course 5% discount fees
3 course 10% discount fees
4 course/More 15% discount fees
Example: If you enroll in Sewing (R1500), First Aid (R1500), and Cooking (R750), your total before discount is R3750. With a 10% discount, you pay R3375.

Privacy Policy

  • Data Collection: We collect personal data (name, contact info, course history) only when voluntarily submitted.
  • Usage: This data is used to manage enrollment, issue certificates, and send relevant course updates or reminders.
  • Security: All data is securely stored and not shared with third parties unless legally required.
  • Opt-Out: Users can unsubscribe from email communications at any time.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies for basic analytics and functionality improvement.

    • For full details, or to request data deletion, please email: info@EmpoweringTheNation.co.za

    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    Q1: Are the courses certified? A: While we do not offer formal SETA certification, learners receive a signed Certificate of Completion for every course.
    Q2: How much do the courses cost? A: Six-month courses are R1500 each, and six-week short courses are R750 each. Discounts apply when enrolling in more than one course.
    Q3: Who can register? A: Anyone over the age of 16. Our training is ideal for domestic workers, gardeners, youth, and unemployed individuals.
    Q4: Can employers pay on behalf of their workers? A: Yes! We encourage employers to support their staff. Receipts and course progress reports are provided.
    Q5: Is training available online? A: Our mobile app and website allow for digital registration, updates, and certain course modules. Full e-learning is in development.
    Q6: Where are the classes held? A: Johannesburg-based classes are held at our community partner venues. Locations vary and are shared at enrollment.

    Terms and Conditions


  • Non-Transferable – Discounts cannot be transferred to another individual or applied to future courses after registration.
  • No Retroactive Discounts – Discounts cannot be applied to courses already paid for in full.
  • Combined Offers – Multi-course discounts cannot be combined with any promotional or seasonal discount offers unless otherwise stated.
  • Refund Policy – If a learner withdraws from a course, the refund will be calculated after removing the discount applied (i.e., standard course fee will apply for completed or retained courses).
  • Limited Time Adjustments – Empowering the Nation reserves the right to revise or suspend this policy during special promotional periods or economic conditions. Notice of changes will be provided.
